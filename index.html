About Us

Established in 1994 by Mr. P.N. Gupta, Bright Metal & Ferro Alloys has built a strong reputation as a reliable partner in the ferro alloys, metals, and minerals industry. With more than three decades of expertise, we have proudly served steel plants, foundries, and various industrial sectors with raw materials that ensure high performance and durability.

Our mission is simple: to deliver quality, consistency, and value. Backed by a customer-first approach, we source and supply materials that meet international standards. Our vision is to continue growing as a leader in the industry, expanding globally while maintaining the trust and satisfaction of our customers.

Our Products

Ferro Alloys

Ferro Silicon

Used as a deoxidizer and alloying element in steelmaking, it improves strength, hardness, and corrosion resistance of steel.

Ferro Manganese

A key deoxidizer and desulfurizer in steel production, it enhances toughness and wear resistance.

Silico Manganese

Combines the properties of silicon and manganese, widely used in steelmaking to improve strength and deoxidation.

Silico Magnesium

Used primarily in SG Iron casting to convert graphite flakes into nodules, improving ductility and toughness of ductile iron.

Ferro Chrome

Essential in stainless steel production, it provides hardness, corrosion resistance, and high-temperature strength.

Ferro Molybdenum

Improves strength, hardenability, and resistance to wear and corrosion in alloy and stainless steels.

Carbon & Additives

Graphite Scrap

A carbon-rich material used in steelmaking and foundries for recarburization and as a conductive additive.

Coal Dust (Steam Coal)

Used as a carbon additive and fuel source in foundries and metallurgical industries.

Silicon Carbide

A hard carbon-based material used as a recarburizer in steelmaking, and as an abrasive or refractory additive.
