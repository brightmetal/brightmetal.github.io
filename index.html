About Us

Established in 1994 by Mr. P.N. Gupta, Bright Metal & Ferro Alloys has built a strong reputation as a reliable partner in the ferro alloys, metals, and minerals industry. With more than three decades of expertise, we have proudly served steel plants, foundries, and various industrial sectors with raw materials that ensure high performance and durability.

Our mission is simple: to deliver quality, consistency, and value. Backed by a customer-first approach, we source and supply materials that meet international standards. Our vision is to continue growing as a leader in the industry, expanding globally while maintaining the trust and satisfaction of our customers.

Our Products

Acidic Ramming Mass

High-quality refractory material used in steel and foundry industries. Ensures durability and heat resistance. Easy to mold and apply in various furnace linings.

Basic Ramming Mass

Specialized refractory for high-temperature applications. Provides thermal stability and long service life. Ideal for ladle and furnace linings.

Graphite Scrap

Pure graphite scrap suitable for electrodes and crucibles. High carbon content ensures excellent conductivity and resistance. Perfect for metallurgical processes.

Ferro Silicon

Essential alloy for steelmaking and cast iron production. Improves strength, hardness, and resistance to oxidation. Supplied in superior quality lumps or powder.

Ferro Manganese

Critical for deoxidizing steel and enhancing hardness. Manufactured with consistent composition. Widely used in steel and foundry industries.

Bentonite Powder

Sodium-based powder for mold binding and casting applications. Ensures excellent plasticity, water retention, and adhesion. Perfect for foundry operations.

Perlite Ore

Lightweight mineral used for insulation and refractory purposes. Expands when heated and provides excellent thermal insulation. Ideal for industrial and construction use.
