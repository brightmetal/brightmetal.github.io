About Us

Established in 1994 by Mr. P.N. Gupta, Bright Metal & Ferro Alloys has built a strong reputation as a reliable partner in the ferro alloys, metals, and minerals industry. With more than three decades of expertise, we have proudly served steel plants, foundries, and various industrial sectors with raw materials that ensure high performance and durability.

Our mission is simple: to deliver quality, consistency, and value. Backed by a customer-first approach, we source and supply materials that meet international standards. Our vision is to continue growing as a leader in the industry, expanding globally while maintaining the trust and satisfaction of our customers.

Our Products

Ferro Alloys

Ferro Silicon

Essential alloy for steelmaking and cast iron production. Improves strength, hardness, and resistance to oxidation.

Ferro Manganese

Critical for deoxidizing steel and enhancing hardness. Widely used in steel and foundry industries.

Silico Manganese

High-strength ferroalloy used in steelmaking. Enhances toughness, wear resistance, and overall durability.

Ferro Chrome

Key alloy for stainless steel production. Provides corrosion resistance, hardness, and strength.

Ferro Molybdenum

Improves strength, hardness, and corrosion resistance in steel. Used in high-strength alloys.

Refractories

Acidic Ramming Mass

High-quality refractory material used in induction furnaces. Ensures durability and heat resistance.

Basic Ramming Mass

Specialized refractory for high-temperature applications. Provides thermal stability and long service life.

Minerals

Bentonite Powder

Sodium-based powder for mold binding and casting. Ensures excellent plasticity and adhesion.

Perlite Ore

Lightweight mineral used for insulation and refractory purposes. Expands when heated.

Fluorspar Powder

Widely used in steelmaking, aluminum, and cement industries. Provides fluxing and desulfurization.

Carbon & Additives

Graphite Scrap

High carbon content material. Used in electrodes, crucibles, and metallurgical processes.

Coal Dust

Steam coal dust used in foundry and metallurgical industries. Provides heat and energy efficiency.
